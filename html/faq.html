General questions

Account registration required?

Account registration at PrepBootstrap is only required if you will be selling or buying themes. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I remove my account?

• Yes, you can. Following belong steps.
• First you go to your account and click on the [Direct removal link] to be directed to the account removal page.
• Click “Yes, Remove my Account.”
• Go to your email and confirm the removal of your account.

is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for themes, templates and other items, including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD

Can I book a room for another person?

If you want to pay for someone else's room, you can absolutely do that. However,
you'll need to make sure that when you enter person details, the name you
put on the booking ticket matches the person’s NIC card exactly. Contact us
if any information needs to be edited after the ticket has been booked.
(011-2222222)

Where can I find my booking confirmation?

If you make a booking from the website, you will receive an e-mail within an
hour. Else, you can contact us. Otherwise, check your credit card statement to
see who charged your card.

Can I change my reservation?

Yes, you can. You have two options.
First, you should do Please check availability at your chosen hotel and that change
your booking.

• Through our website: firstly, you should do cancel your previous
reservation before making a new reservation.
• Through the hotel directly: you can find contact details on the hotel’s
contact page.

are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
