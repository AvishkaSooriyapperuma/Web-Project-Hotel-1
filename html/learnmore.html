Pressed into a little island about the size of the State of Florida, every fascination and city of interest are only a bounce and a skirt away from one another.
Seashores are the fundamental fascination of Sri Lanka and the most loved are the ones found in the Southern corner of the nation. The Golden Mile Beach which spreads among Bentota and Beruwala offers experiences in the sea from scuba plunging and swimming to kite surfing, water skiing, and surfing. With the most profound wreck jump and a portion of the picturesque plunging area arranged close to a choice plunging supplies and preparing focuses, make the Golden Mile Beach an advantageous area to satisfy your requirement for water experience.

The shallow sandy seashores of Weligama Bay is an ideal area to get your first wave.
